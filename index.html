Leveraging computer-aided design (CAD) and cutting-edge manufacturing techniques, I not only refined prototypes but also fostered close collaboration with the company founder to transform abstract concepts into tangible, marketable products. These experiences underscore my commitment to bridging theoretical knowledge with practical application, highlighting my dedication to engineering innovation. Moreover, my proficiency shines through projects like creating a miniature pneumatic engine, designing wooden furniture, and joining Formula SAE which reflect my hands-on skills and unwavering passion for applying engineering expertise in tangible ways.

Throughout MIE243, various "Dissections" were conducted. These involved being given a mechanical device, taking it apart, and then modelling it in Solidworks. The first dissection was a hand-draft of a Ball Bearing.
